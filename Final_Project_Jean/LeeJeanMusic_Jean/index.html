About Me

Motivated by music at the age of 9 when my parents bought my first electric guitar.
Since then I have wanted to perform and got some inspiration from my late brother to sieze
the moment and go after what I love. After winning a spot on American Idol's Farewell Season
on Fox, I finished at the top 8. Since then I have been finishing school and playing at local gigs
and honing my craft. Writing music and preparing to enter the studio and deliver a product
that will entertain and please the ears.
